Provii lets you prove your age without revealing your date of birth. When a website or service needs to confirm you are old enough, Provii generates a zero knowledge proof on your device. The service learns one fact: you meet the age requirement. Nothing else.

Your credential lives on your phone, protected by biometric authentication. There is no central database storing your identity. No server ever sees your date of birth during verification. The cryptography ensures that even Provii itself cannot link your activity back to you as an individual.

Getting started is straightforward. Visit an authorised Issuing Party, verify your identity once, and receive your age credential. From that point on, any participating website or service can verify your age instantly. No photos of your ID. No manual checks. No personal data shared.

Provii works with businesses and organisations across Australia that need age verification. Pubs, bottle shops, online retailers, gaming platforms. Anywhere age matters, Provii gives you a faster way through while keeping your personal information where it belongs: with you.
